Boutique Fitness Studio, South Florida

45% Less Admin Time
The Problem: 8+ hours/week on scheduling conflicts, member billing issues, staff coordination. Members were churning because of scheduling confusion and billing errors.

What Changed: We built a unified member dashboard with auto-reminders, smart scheduling that shows staff availability, and integrated billing that actually works.

The Result: 8 hours/week became 4.5 hours/week (45% reduction). Member churn dropped 18%. That's 25+ extra members staying per year = $30k-50k more annual revenue. Total cost: $3k/month. ROI: Pays for itself month 1.

Upscale Salon, Los Angeles

$48k Revenue Recovered
The Problem: 35% no-show rate costing $4k/month in lost revenue. Clients forgetting appointments. No follow-up system. Inventory was manual nightmare.

What Changed: Automated SMS/email reminders 24h and 2h before appointments. Deposit requirement for chronic no-shows. Client portal showing their service history.

The Result: No-show rate dropped to 8%. That's $3k-4k/month revenue recovered. Plus 10 hours/week saved on admin. First year value: $48k+ in recovered revenue alone. Cost: $2.5k/month.
