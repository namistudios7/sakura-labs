Real Results

Boutique Hotel in South Florida

45% efficiency gain
The Challenge: Manual booking management, scattered customer data, no automated reminders or follow-ups. Front desk staff spent 3 hours daily on administrative work.

We built a centralized guest management system with automated check-in reminders, integrated payment processing, and a simple dashboard for staff to manage bookings, cancellations, and upsells. Built in 4 weeks, training took 1 day.

Result: 3 hours of daily admin work cut to 90 minutes. No new hires needed. Customers got faster service. Revenue stayed the same, margin improved.

Fitness Studio in Miami

60% faster client onboarding
The Challenge: New members were dropping off because onboarding was clunky. Trainers were using their phones to track client progress. Billing was a nightmare (spreadsheets + manual invoicing).

We built a member portal (progress tracking, workout history, nutrition guides), automated billing with payment reminders, and a trainer dashboard that pulls everything together. Integrated with their existing payment processor.

Result: 30-day member retention increased from 65% to 85%. Trainers saved 5 hours/week on admin. Billing errors dropped to near zero. They grew without hiring admin staff.

Digital Marketing Consultant (Solo Practitioner)

10 hours/week saved
The Challenge: Juggling client projects across multiple tools (email, Slack, spreadsheets). Couldn't track who was paying, who needed follow-ups, or where projects stood. Invoicing was manual. Time tracking was non-existent.

We built a simple project management + billing dashboard. Clients can log in to see project status, deliverables, and invoice history. Automated reminders for late payments. Built-in time tracking. Everything synced.

Result: 10 hours/week saved on admin. Could take on 30% more clients without hiring. Revenue per month increased $4k with zero additional headcount.

Boutique Spa in Los Angeles

40% reduction in no-shows
The Challenge: No-shows were costing them $2k/month in lost revenue. Client data was scattered across multiple notebooks and emails. Inventory tracking (lotions, oils, supplies) was manual and error-prone.

We built an appointment system with automated SMS reminders (24h before, 2h before), customer profiles with service history and preferences, and inventory management that flags when supplies are low. Simple staff dashboard.

Result: No-show rate dropped 40%. Clients felt more personalized service. Inventory ordering became efficient (cut waste by 25%). Operations smoother with the same team.
